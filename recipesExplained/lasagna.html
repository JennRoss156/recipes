Maine Lobster Lasagna

Maine is known for lobster. This recipe will have your tastebuds dancing for joy. Layered with chunks of Maine lobster, alfredo sauce, fresh spinach, and three types of cheese. Serve with a nice side salad or soup and oven-toasted garlic bread.

Recipe by:ShelakUpdated January 29, 2023

Ingredients Needed

  1. 1 (15 ounce) container of ricotta cheese
  2. 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  3. 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  5. 2 large eggs
  7. 1 medium onion, minced
  8. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  9. 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  10. 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  11. 2 (16 ounce) jar of alfredo pasta sauce
  12. 16 no-boil lasagna noodles
  13. 2 pounds cooked and cubed lobster meat
  14. 1 (10 ounce) package baby spinach leaves

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

Step 2

Place ricotta cheese into a large bowl. Stir in 1 cup cheddar, 1/2 cup mozzarella, 1/2 cup parmesan, and eggs. Mix in onion, parsley, garlic, and pepper.

Step 3

Spread 1 1/2 cups of alfredo sauce over the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Top with a layer of 4 lasagna noodles. Arrange 1/3 of the lobster meat over the noodles, then cover with 1/3 of spinach and another layer of of alfredo sauce. Repeat these steps as you layer twice more: noodles, lobster meat, spinach, then sauce. Sprinkle remaining cheddar, mozzarella, and parmesan over the top. Give the whole a gentle shake just to get the ingredients settled in.

Step 4

Cover the baking dish loosely with foil and bake for 45 minutes once oven si preheated. Remove foil and continue baking until cheese is browned (about 10 minutes).

Step 5

Allow lasagna to cool for 10 minutes before serving. ENjoy!
